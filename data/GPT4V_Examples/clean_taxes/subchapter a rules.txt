17 NCAC 01A .0101	LOCATION
The North Carolina Department of Revenue is located in the Revenue Building, 501 N. Wilmington Street, Raleigh, North Carolina.  The mailing address is P.O. Box 25000, Raleigh, North Carolina 27640.
