17 NCAC 01C .0102	SECRETARY DEFINED
When used in this Title, "secretary" shall mean the Secretary of Revenue of the State of North Carolina.

History Note:	Authority G.S. 105-262; 143B‑220;
Eff. February 1, 1976;
Pursuant to G.S. 150B-21.3A, rule is necessary without substantive public interest Eff. August 20, 2016.
